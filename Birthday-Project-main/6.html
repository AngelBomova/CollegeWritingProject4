Drawbacks: Over-reliance on AI

While AI has many advantages, we should not rely solely on it. Over reliance on AI could make us less critical thinkers and more dependent on technology to make important decisions for us. This can be shown in "smart" cities. These cities display everything that AI controls, from traffic lights to waste. Although these technologies can bring efficiency up, they also make us quite vulnerable to technical failures and cyberattacks. Furthermore, we are addicted to smartphones and devices powered by AI, which raises serious privacy concerns. Everything we do, like using social media, shopping online, or searching the web, is tracked and stored as data by AI systems. Eventually, this information will be used to create highly personalized ads which is a direct invasion of privacy. The more our dependency on such devices, the larger the extent to which AI takes over our lives. This can lead to infringements of privacy and can even go as far as manipulation.
