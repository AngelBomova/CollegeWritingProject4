The Rapid Rise of AI: Government and Societal Integration

Over the last decade, AI has become widespread and rampant. AI systems serve for governments and corporations to increase the quality of services, make decisions, and automate tasks. It has become so integrated in people's lives that many are not even aware of the frequency with which they deal with it. From virtual personal assistants like Siri and Alexa, to GPS systems and recommendation algorithms on social media, there is pretty much AI everywhere. AI is being applied to healthcare for the analysis of medical data, education to create personalized learning, and finance to predict future market trends. It does so in such a manner, the growth of AI has made it an essential part of our lives, with limitless applications. Yet, with the rapid advancements in AI, it is important to remain concerned and aware about the risks it poses as it becomes more integrated into our daily lives.
