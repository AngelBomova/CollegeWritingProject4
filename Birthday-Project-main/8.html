Mitigating the Adverse Impact of AI

Despite these challenges, it's not too late to mitigate the negative effects of AI. The rapid development of AI needs to be slowed down. This is necessary to regulate and ensure that AI serves ethical means. Governments and industries should prioritize policies to protect workers, such as retraining programs and financial assistance to support those affected by automation. On top of this, ethics frameworks should be developed to ensure the technology works for the majority, not just for the few powerful corporations. AI should be used to enhance human labor, rather than replace it.
