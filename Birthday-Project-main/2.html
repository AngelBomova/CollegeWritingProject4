Introduction: The Rise of Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence, AI, refers to the development of machines or systems designed to think and learn like human beings. From self-driving cars to advanced robotics, AI has huge potential for significantly enhancing many aspects of daily life. It can quickly process large volumes of data, make decisions in real-time, and perform tasks that were thought to be uniquely reserved for human beings. AI can also improve productivity in making lives more convenient and offer personalized experiences that once seemed unimaginable.

If we are not careful, AI might become so great that it could threaten to destroy jobs, challenge privacy, and even alter the very tenor of our society in ways we're unprepared to handle. As we advance forward in developing these technologies, it is important to reflect on how to use it ethically, ensuring we do not allow AI to reshape what it means to be human on behalf of the people it's allegedly serving.
